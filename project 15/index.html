Get to know more about TEMU

Temu is an online shopping platform launched in 2022, operating as a subsidiary of PDD Holdings Inc., the parent company of China's popular e-commerce platform Pinduoduo. Headquartered in Boston, Temu connects consumers directly with manufacturers, primarily based in China, offering a wide array of products such as clothing, electronics, and household items at competitive prices.

Global Expansion

Since its U.S. debut in September 2022, Temu has rapidly expanded its reach. By early 2023, it ventured into markets including Canada, Australia, New Zealand, and several European countries such as France, Germany, and the United Kingdom. In July 2023, Temu entered the Asian market, launching in Japan and South Korea, and continued its expansion into Southeast Asia with its entry into the Philippines and Malaysia by August 2023.
